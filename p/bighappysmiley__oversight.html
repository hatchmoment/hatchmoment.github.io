Oversight

Oversight – parental control suite to block apps/websites and monitor usage

notablePython✦ Apps & Productivity

It lets parents manage and limit their child's device usage across apps and websites. A Node.js/Express backend stores policies, a React PWA provides a dashboard, and Python agents enforce limits, block sites, and report usage on macOS, Windows, and Linux. The system is self‑hosted, giving families full control without relying on third‑party services. Compared to generic screen‑time tools, it offers cross‑device enforcement, customizable policies, and open‑source transparency.
